About Ajira Hub

Ajira Hub is a digital platform developed by The Groupchat Think Tank to address youth unemployment in Rwanda and East Africa. It connects unemployed youth with career opportunities in agriculture and tourism through mentorship, job listings, and investor networks.

Our Mission

To empower young people with the tools, resources, and support systems they need to build sustainable careers in key growth sectors like tourism and agriculture.

Our Vision

A thriving generation of African youth empowered with access to mentorship, meaningful jobs, and investor networks.

How Ajira Hub Helps

  • Smart job-matching for youth and companies
  • Mentorship by experienced professionals
  • Networking with local and global investors
  • Workshops, online training, and e-learning
