What is Ajira Hub?

Ajira Hub is a digital platform designed by The Groupchat Think Tank to connect unemployed youth with meaningful opportunities in agriculture, tourism, and digital careers. We aim to drive transformation through innovation, mentorship, and access to jobs in key sectors.
